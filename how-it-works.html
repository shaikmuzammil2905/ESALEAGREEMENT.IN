How It Works

An interactive 8-step timeline showcasing the workflow from registration to downloading secure documents.

1

1. Register Business / Individual

Sign up in seconds. Enter your business details, Aadhaar, PAN/GST number, and set up your organization workspace.

2

2. Complete Aadhaar eKYC

Securely submit and verify your Aadhaar identity directly using government-linked eKYC validation systems.

3

3. Select Buyer or Seller

Assign legal roles. Pick whether you are drafting the agreement as a Buyer, Seller, or trusted intermediary.

4

4. Verify The Other Party

Send verification requests. The opposing participant registers and verifies their identity via Aadhaar & GST check.

5

5. Add Product Details & Upload Images

Complete property descriptions, cost break-ups, transaction rules, and upload high-resolution verification images.

6

6. Review The Agreement

Read the final terms and conditions generated. Make modifications and verify clauses before locking the draft.

7

7. Aadhaar eSign by Both Parties

Complete authentication. Both parties sign securely via government eSign portals using Aadhaar OTP verification.

8

8. Download & Store Securely

Finalization. Download the legal PDF featuring digital certificates, tamper seals, and audit documentation logs.
